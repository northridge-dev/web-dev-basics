Middle Schooler Wins Design Contest

Design champ credits his "unbelievably amazing" coding teacher.

For the first time ever, a middle school student has been honored with a prestigious Webby Award. The award is typically reserved for professional designers and UX experts, but the judges couldn't resist the whimsical charm of the 12-year-old's submission.

"From his first day in my class, I knew he was special," beamed his proud coding teacher. "He's got a real knack for this stuff and a truckload of talent."
